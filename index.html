The Coffee Shop Reunion

Interactive ESL Lesson by Maroua

Key Vocabulary

Ambiance (بيئة / جو عام)
  • The cafe has a very relaxing ambiance.
  • I love the cozy ambiance of this local cafe.
  • Lighting plays a huge role in creating the right ambiance.
  • The soft jazz music really adds to the ambiance.
Specialty Coffee (قهوة مختصة)
  • They only serve specialty coffee from Ethiopia.
  • I've become a fan of specialty coffee lately.
  • The barista is an expert in specialty coffee brewing.
  • This is the best specialty coffee I've ever had.
Catch up (تبادل الأخبار)
  • Let’s meet for coffee to catch up.
  • It was great to catch up with you!
  • We spent three hours just catching up.
  • I need to catch up with my sister this weekend.

Useful Expressions

"It's been ages!" (مر وقت طويل جداً!)
  • Hi! It's been ages since we met!
  • I can't believe it, it's been ages!
  • How are you? It's been ages!
  • We must talk soon, it's been ages!
"This is my treat." (هذا على حسابي)
  • Put your wallet away; this is my treat.
  • Let's go eat, this is my treat.
  • I'm paying for the coffee; this is my treat.
  • Since you're visiting me, this is my treat.

Practice with Your Photos

1. Describe the Ambiance

Start with: "The men are enjoying the..."
Answer: The men are enjoying the professional ambiance of the cafe while they catch up on work.

2. Making Coffee

Start with: "The barista is preparing..."
Answer: The barista is preparing specialty coffee. The aroma must be wonderful!

3. Happy Friends

Start with: "The two friends are..."
Answer: The two friends are reminiscing about old memories and catching up!
